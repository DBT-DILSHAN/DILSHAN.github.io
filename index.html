05 — NOTES

Field Notes

VFD Fault-Finding

Most Altivar drive trips trace back to three things: an overheating motor, a loose power terminal, or a wrong parameter setting. Check these before assuming the drive itself is faulty.

Panel Safety Habit

Always test your tester before and after checking for dead. A meter that reads zero on a dead circuit only means something if you know it works on a live one too.

Motor Control Tip

Contactor chatter is usually a coil voltage problem, not a mechanical one. Check supply voltage stability at the coil before replacing the contactor.
